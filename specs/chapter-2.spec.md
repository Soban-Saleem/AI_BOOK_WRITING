3.  **Section 3: From Author to Architect: A New Role for Writers (Approx. 300 words)**
    *   Discuss the shift in the writer's role from a "prose generator" to a "content architect".
    *   Emphasize that this new role elevates the writer, allowing them to focus on high-level strategy, creativity, and domain expertise, while the AI handles the laborious task of generating the text.
